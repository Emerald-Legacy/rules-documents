Rotated Cards

All the cards listed here rotated out of the valid card pool and are not allowed to be used in official Emerald Legacy tournaments. Unlike Banned cards, which might be unbanned at some point, rotated cards will never return to the valid card pool.

Rotated are all cards of the original FFG Core Set as well as the complete Imperial Cycle. In addition, the following cards are rotated because they were replaced with slightly different cards in Restoration of Balance.
